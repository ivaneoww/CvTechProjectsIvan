Los Angeles Azules



Angeles Azules is a Mexican band who play record breaking cumbias such as "Te Nescesito" & "Mis Sentimientos". Every Spanish speaking family has listened to atleast one of their songs. They've been a band since the 1980's and they continue to go on tour replaying their well known songs.
